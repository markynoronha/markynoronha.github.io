Continuing on with a similar theme to the original Air Jordan 4 Thunder, the Air Jordan 4 Red Thunder features an all-black Durabuck upper with red netted underlays and eyelets. Released in January 2022 at a retail price of $190, The Air Jordan 4 Red Thunder is a staple of any sneakerheads collection.

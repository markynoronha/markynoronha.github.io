Description
The Yeezy 700 Boost Wave Runner was the trailblazer for the Yeezy brand's shift from its slim, sleek, minimal aesthetic to a chunky, 90s inspired one. The popularity of the 700 beautifully mixes aspects from knit runners like the 350 and the robust skate kicks like the 550. The Yeezy 700 Wave Runner is crafted with grey mesh and upper leather, with black suede overlays and teal mesh underlays. Its finished off with the classic thick Boost sole, with mixing in spots of orange to create contrast and flair. Although riginally released in November 2017, the Yeezy Boost 700 Wave Runner was recently re-released in March of 2022 at a retail price of $300.

Virgil Abloh (RIP), has followed up his release of the ultra-successful "The Ten" project with Nike, with another masterpiece addition to the Nike Air Presto Off-White. Sticking to the industrial style, this pair features a dominating white colorway. To add contrast, orange detailing and a grey Nike Swoosh are stitched onto the side panel, "AIR" is written on the side of the heel in black, and a white Nike tag appears on the tongue. The Nike Air Presto Off-White 'White' dropped in June 2018 at a retail price of $160, remaining one of the hottest pairs out since then.
